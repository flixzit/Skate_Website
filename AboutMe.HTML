About Me

Hi, I'm Palowski, the founder of Palowski Skateworks. I've been passionate about skateboarding for over 12 years. Whether it's performing tricks, building custom boards, or just enjoying the ride, skateboarding has always been a way for me to express myself and connect with others.

My goal with Palowski Skateworks is to share my love for the sport and help others improve their skills, while also offering a community for skateboarders to connect and share their experiences.
